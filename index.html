Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the content-automation application ("the App") operated by Mr Knowledge Share. The App is a personal tool used solely to publish the operator's own content to the operator's own social media accounts. It is not offered as a service to other users and has no third-party end users.

Information We Collect

The App accesses only the operator's own connected accounts (such as Pinterest) via official platform APIs, using access tokens the operator authorizes. It does not collect, request, or process personal data from any other individuals.

How Information Is Used

Access tokens are used exclusively to publish posts (for example, creating a Pinterest Pin that links to a YouTube video) to the operator's own accounts. Tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine and are never shared, sold, or transmitted to third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. The only data transmitted is the content the operator chooses to publish, sent directly to the relevant platform's official API.

Data Retention

Authorization tokens are retained locally only as long as needed to operate the App and can be revoked at any time by the operator through the respective platform's settings (e.g. Pinterest account security settings).

Third-Party Platforms

The App interacts with third-party platforms (Pinterest, YouTube, and others) through their official APIs. Use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies and terms of service.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
